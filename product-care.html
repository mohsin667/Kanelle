Product Care

At Kanelle, we believe in longevity and timeless design. All our products are handcrafted to last, using natural materials and expert craftsmanship. But the way we wash and care for our garments is just as important. No two fibres are the same, and each requires its own method of washing and storing.

All our products are colour tested and we mention specifications of wash care for each product. Pls read the instructions of washing carefully on the product page along with the tags mentioned on the garment that say dry clean only.
