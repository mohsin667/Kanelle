Our Process

Over the years, Kanelle has been collaborating with craftspeople such as weavers, print makers, dyers & embroiders to produce unique and original pieces that are beautifully handmade.Our products are carefully designed and crafted with age old techniques such as block printing, hand embroidery and textile weaving. Some of the visual content is to bring to you the transparency in our processes and show you how our inspiration is derived.

Kanelle is on a journey to create a more thoughtful way of being in this world. We believe that everyone should be treated with fairness and thoughtfulness. We have longstanding relationships with many of our makers and the social conscience team at Kanelle works closely with our suppliers to ensure that they follow our ethical policies.

About us

Kanelle is a label, created for women who like wasy-to-wear fashion. The collections explore timeless separates, comprising of meticulous details through an innovative approach. With the use of handcrafted indigenous textiles. kanelle embraces wardrobe staples that are feminine while being unique and wearable.
