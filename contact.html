Kanelle is a label created for women who like easy-to-wear fashion. The collections explore timeless separates comprising of meticulous details added through an innovative approach. With the use of handcrafted indigenous textiles, Kanelle features wardrobe staples that are feminine, individualistic, and easily wearable.
